Rinaldi Landscaping Service

About Us

Rinaldi Landscaping Service was started in 1989 by Dr. Jay Rinaldi & his son Peter Rinaldi in Brunswick, New Jersey. Dr. Rinaldi, a noted arborist, created the business initially to care for damaged or sick trees, but gradually this evolved into the full service landscaping company it is today! The Rinaldi's take pride in what they do and it shows in their meticulous attention to detail and desire to give you the lawn you've always dreamed of!!

All In The Family

Dr. Rinaldi's legacy continues through the work of his son, as well as, his grandsons: Peter Jr., Markus, and Timothy. Rinaldi Landscaping Service is proud to be a family owned business for over 27 years!
